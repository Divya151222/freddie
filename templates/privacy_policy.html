Privacy Policy for EDZ Coach – Freddie Intelligent Life Coach

Effective Date: November 1, 2024
Last Updated: November 3, 2024

Welcome to Freddie, an intelligent life coach platform by EDZ Informatics, accessible through https://www.edzcoach.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use Google Login to access Freddie.

We value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your information in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Google Login to access Freddie, we collect and store the following information from your Google account:

  • Name: Your full name as provided in your Google profile.
  • Email: Your email to verify your credentials to log in.

No other data from your Google account is accessed or collected.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data solely to enhance your experience with Freddie and personalize the services we provide:

  • Name: To personalize interactions with Freddie.
  • Email: To verify your credentials to log in and send you the transcript of the session with Freddie (Intelligent online Life coach).

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • Your data is securely stored in Azure SQL databases.
  • Data is retained for a period of 12 months from your last login.
  • After 6 months of inactivity, your data will be permanently deleted from our systems.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your data to any third parties. Your data is used exclusively within EDZ Coach for the purpose of delivering our services.

5. Data Security

  • Encryption of data at rest and in transit.
  • Regular security audits and compliance checks.
  • Restricted access to the database, limited to authorized personnel only.

6. User Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You may request to view the data we have collected about you.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data at any time.
  • Correction: You can update or correct your data if it is inaccurate.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hnag343@gmail.com.

7. Use of Google API Services

Freddie uses Google API services to authenticate users and retrieve specific data (Name, Email). Our use of Google APIs complies with Google’s User Data Policy. By using Freddie, you consent to our access and use of your data as described in this policy.

For more details about Google’s data practices, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

8. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
